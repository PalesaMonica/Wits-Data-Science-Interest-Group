# Data Cleaning Overview

Data cleaning is the process of **identifying and correcting errors or inconsistencies in a dataset** to ensure accurate and reliable analysis.  

In this section, we focus on:

- **Checking and handling missing values** (MCAR, MAR, MNAR)  
- **Correcting datatypes** for numeric, categorical, and datetime columns  
- **Identifying and removing duplicates**  
- **Detecting and handling outliers**  
- **Standardizing formatting** for consistency  

By the end of this section, you will understand how to **prepare real-world messy data** for analysis and modeling.
